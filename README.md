\# 📚 NyayaSetu — Legal AI RAG Platform



NyayaSetu is a production-grade Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) system designed for Indian legal intelligence.  

It enables semantic legal search, grounded legal reasoning, and citation-aware answers across large volumes of constitutional and judicial documents.



---



\## 🚀 Features



• Semantic legal search across 280+ Indian legal PDFs  

• LangChain-based semantic chunking with contextual overlap  

• Offline transformer embeddings for zero-cost indexing  

• Pinecone cloud vector memory  

• Gemini powered grounded legal reasoning  

• FastAPI microservice for real-time legal AI inference  

• Citation-aware, low-hallucination legal responses
